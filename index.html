Company Overview

Building the Foundation for Future E-Waste Innovation

Wrek’d Tech, LLC is a founder-led South Carolina green-tech startup focused on future electronic waste innovation, circular economy development, compliance-readiness infrastructure, and R&D collaboration pathways.

The company is currently building its controlled business and administrative foundation, advancing early-stage compliance-readiness work, and preparing exploratory pathways for future academic, agency, and technical collaboration.

Wrek’d Tech does not currently operate a recycling facility, ITAD facility, lab, warehouse, office, or commercial processing site. The company’s current posture is business-readiness, administrative-readiness, R2 Ready alpha development, Ex-Spec validation-readiness, and outreach restart.

South Carolina Development Posture

Rooted in South Carolina. Designed for Future Circular Innovation.

Wrek’d Tech is currently founder-led from North Charleston, South Carolina. Orangeburg, South Carolina is designated as the company’s future headquarters location as Wrek’d Tech’s collaboration, administrative, and development pathway matures.

This South Carolina-centered posture supports Wrek’d Tech’s long-range interest in circular economy research, future e-waste innovation, workforce development, and academic collaboration within the state.

Ex-Spec Concept

Ex-Spec: A Conceptual Advanced E-Waste Recovery System

Ex-Spec is Wrek’d Tech’s conceptual advanced e-waste recovery system under validation-readiness planning.

The concept is intended to support future research into smarter electronic waste processing, material recovery, data-informed recycling workflows, and circular economy infrastructure. Ex-Spec has no physical prototype at present and will require R&D collaboration, technical validation, and phased proof-of-concept development before operational or commercial deployment.

R2 Ready: Supporting Compliance-Readiness Work

R2 Ready is Wrek’d Tech’s active alpha-stage compliance-readiness initiative focused on R2 readiness preparation, structured compliance workflow development, evidence organization, and future pilot validation.

R2 Ready does not certify facilities, guarantee certification, replace auditors or Certification Bodies, override certification processes, or represent SERI or any Certification Body.

R2 Ready details will be published separately as the initiative matures.
