Company Overview

Building the Foundation for Future E-Waste Innovation

Wrek’d Tech, LLC is a founder-led South Carolina green-tech startup focused on future electronic waste innovation, circular economy development, compliance-readiness infrastructure, and R&D collaboration pathways.

Flagship Concept

Ex-Spec: A Conceptual Advanced E-Waste Recovery System

Ex-Spec is Wrek’d Tech’s conceptual advanced e-waste recovery system under validation-readiness planning.

The concept is intended to support future research into smarter electronic waste processing, material recovery, data-informed recycling workflows, and circular economy infrastructure.

Ex-Spec has no physical prototype at present and will require R&D collaboration, technical validation, and phased proof-of-concept development before operational or commercial deployment.

Conceptual and validation-readiness stage. No prototype exists today; Ex-Spec is presented as a future R&D and validation opportunity, not a validated, deployed, or operational technology.
