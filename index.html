Flagship Wrek’d Tech Initiative

Ex-Spec is a conceptual platform, not the whole company.

Ex-Spec is Wrek’d Tech’s flagship conceptual advanced e-waste recovery platform under validation-readiness planning.

Ex-Spec is intended to guide future research into smarter electronic waste processing, material recovery, sensing, data-informed recycling workflows, and circular economy infrastructure.

Wrek’d Tech is not proposing that a university build the full Ex-Spec system at this stage. The best initial collaboration pathway is a bounded research workstream inside the platform: material identification, characterization, and recovery feasibility.

Conceptual and validation-readiness stage only. No prototype exists today; Ex-Spec is not a validated, deployed, operational, or commercial technology.

R2 Ready supports the broader responsible recycling posture.

R2 Ready is Wrek’d Tech’s active alpha-stage compliance-readiness initiative focused on R2 readiness preparation, structured compliance workflow development, evidence organization, and future pilot validation.

It is separate from the Ex-Spec research workstream and remains secondary to the current R&D collaboration positioning of the landing page.

R2 Ready does not certify facilities, guarantee certification, replace auditors or Certification Bodies, override certification processes, or represent SERI or any Certification Body.
